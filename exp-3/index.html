About VES Institute of Technology

VES Institute of Technology is an integral part of VES Institutions, which is a name synonymous with student empowerment through excellence in higher education. VES Institute of Technology, started in 1968, is affiliated to Mumbai University, Mumbai and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and has NBA accredited courses.
